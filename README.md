# Cookie Clicker

A classic incremental game built from scratch using HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. The objective is simple: bake as many cookies as you can!

![Cookie Clicker Gameplay](https://i.imgur.com/Y8v3s5W.png)

## How to Play

1.  **Click the giant cookie:** Every click on the big cookie in the center of the screen earns you one cookie.
2.  **Buy Upgrades:** Use your hard-earned cookies to buy buildings from the store on the right. These buildings automatically generate cookies for you over time.
3.  **Increase Your CPS:** Each upgrade increases your "cookies per second" (CPS) rate. The more upgrades you buy, the faster your cookie count will rise!
4.  **Watch Your Empire Grow:** Keep an eye on the news ticker at the top for humorous updates and milestones related to your cookie empire.

## Features

-   **Endless Clicking Fun:** The primary game mechanic is satisfying and simple.
-   **Automated Production:** Purchase various buildings to automate your cookie production:
    -   **Auto-Clicker:** For when your fingers get tired.
    -   **Grandma:** A sweet old lady who bakes cookies with love.
    -   **Factory:** Mass-produces cookies on an industrial scale.
    -   **Mine:** Unearths delicious chocolate chips and cookie dough.
    -   **Shipment:** Launches cookies from distant galaxies.
-   **Real-time Counters:** The UI displays your total cookie count and your cookies-per-second (CPS) rate, updating in real-time.
-   **Dynamic Store:** Upgrades in the store will light up and become available for purchase as soon as you have enough cookies. The cost of each upgrade increases after every purchase.
-   **Humorous News Ticker:** A scrolling ticker at the top of the screen provides random, funny news headlines related to the world's new cookie economy.
-   **Classic Design:** The styling, layout, and color scheme are heavily inspired by the original Cookie Clicker game for a nostalgic feel.

## Technologies Used

This project is a demonstration of a front-end application built with core web technologies, without the use of any external frameworks or libraries.

-   **HTML5**
-   **CSS3** (with Google Fonts for typography)
-   **JavaScript (ES6+)**
